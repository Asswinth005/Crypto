Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our referral program.

How much can I earn per referral?

You earn $50 in BTC for each friend who signs up using your referral link and completes their first trade of $100 or more. There's no limit to how many friends you can refer!

When do I receive my referral rewards?

Rewards are credited to your account within 24 hours after your referred friend completes their qualifying trade. You can track the status of each referral in your dashboard.

What are the referral tiers and benefits?

We offer three tiers: Bronze (1-4 referrals), Silver (5-19 referrals), and Gold (20+ referrals). Higher tiers unlock bonus percentages, exclusive rewards, and VIP support.

Can I refer friends from any country?

Yes! Our referral program is available globally, except in countries where cryptocurrency trading is restricted by law. Please check our terms for specific country restrictions.

How do I share my referral link?

Once you're logged in, visit the Earn page to get your unique referral link. You can copy the link or use our social sharing buttons to share it directly on Facebook, Twitter, WhatsApp, or Telegram.
